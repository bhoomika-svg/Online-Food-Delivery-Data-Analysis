# 🍔 Online Food Delivery Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes an online food delivery dataset to uncover insights into customer ordering behavior, restaurant performance, and delivery operations.

An interactive dashboard was built to help stakeholders understand key business metrics and make data-driven decisions to improve customer experience and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze order patterns across weekdays and weekends
* Identify top-performing restaurants and cuisines
* Evaluate delivery and food preparation times
* Understand customer ratings and satisfaction

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Orders:** 1898
* **Total Revenue:** $31K
* **Average Rating:** 4.34 ⭐
* **Average Delivery Time:** 24 mins
* **Average Food Preparation Time:** 27 mins

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📅 Order Trends

* ~71% of orders occur on weekends
* Indicates higher demand during non-working days

### 🍱 Cuisine Analysis

* American cuisine generates the highest revenue
* Japanese and Italian cuisines are also highly popular

### 🏆 Top Restaurants

* Shake Shack leads in total orders
* Other top performers include The Meatball Shop and Blue Ribbon Sushi

### ⏱️ Operational Insights

* Delivery and preparation times are consistent
* Potential opportunity to reduce delays and improve efficiency

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization
* **Excel / Python (optional)** – Data cleaning & preprocessing
* **EDA** – Exploratory Data Analysis

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset contains:

* Order ID and Customer ID
* Restaurant name
* Cuisine type
* Order cost
* Day of the week (Weekday/Weekend)
* Customer rating
* Food preparation time
* Delivery time
